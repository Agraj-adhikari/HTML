Chocolate Chip Cookies

The best chewey chocolate chip cookies you'll ever make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 2 cups of choclocate chips
  • 1 tbsp of baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F(175°C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Beat the butter and sugar together in another bowl.
  4. Combine the dry and well ingredients.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
